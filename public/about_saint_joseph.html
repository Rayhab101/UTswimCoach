Saint Joseph's Swim Team

The Saint Joseph's swim team had a great 5th season while swimming with the Ben Lomond High School team. While the student population of the school is small itself, the team had one of their largest groups in the history of the program (10). Each individual excelled in many of the events and most of the school records were broken.\n\n Congratulations to Senior Blake Hanson in medaling in the 50yd Freestyle and beating the 100yd Butterfly record at the 3A State Championships on February 13th, 2020.
